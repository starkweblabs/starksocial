**Voice in one line:** Stark is the agency that tells you the truth, then does the work.

**Key voice rules:**
- Direct, no filler, no "we're passionate about..."
- Proof over promises — lead with results
- Human, not brochure — contractions fine, short sentences fine
- "We" not "I" — team language
- Never: "leverage", "synergy", "best in class", "in today's digital landscape"
- CTAs: action verb first — "Get a proposal", "See our work", "Start here"
