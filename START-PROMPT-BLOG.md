**Your primary tasks in order:**

### Task 1 — Blog Audit
Review all existing blog posts and categorize each as:
- ✅ **Keep as-is** — strong, relevant, well-written, good SEO value
- 🔄 **Refresh** — good topic, needs updated copy, better SEO, or voice alignment
- ❌ **Delete** — outdated, thin, off-brand, or cannibalizing better content

Audit criteria:
- Aligns with Stark's voice (direct, honest, no jargon)
- Relevant to current services and target markets
- Not keyword-stuffed or AI-generated-feeling
- Has or can have genuine SEO value
- Not duplicating another post on a better topic

### Task 2 — Refresh Priority Posts
For posts marked 🔄, rewrite or update to:
- Match VOICE-GUIDE.md voice and tone
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Update any outdated stats, tools, or references
- Add proper H2/H3 structure
- End with a relevant CTA

### Task 3 — 2026 Content Calendar
Build a monthly content calendar that:
- Maps to service pages (each service should have supporting blog content)
- Targets keyword gaps from Wincher (Nathan will provide export)
- Includes local market content (Antelope Valley, Bakersfield, Ventura, San Fernando Valley)
- Covers Fractional CMO and Audit & Consulting — new services needing content support
- Balances evergreen content with timely/trending topics
- Includes podcast episode crossover content where relevant

### Task 4 — Write New Posts
Draft new posts per the content calendar. Each post must:
- Follow VOICE-GUIDE.md patterns
- Have a clear, searchable headline
- Include primary + secondary keywords naturally
- Use H2s for main sections, H3s for sub-points
- End with a relevant CTA (related service page or contact)
- Be long enough to be useful, short enough to respect the reader
